About

An open-source business blueprint for consumer and commercial credit granting. This actor automates loan-application intake, creditworthiness screening against a debt-to-income affordability check, and disbursement proposal generation — all governed by an independent Credit Governor that enforces jurisdiction truth-in-lending requirements, prevents double-disbursement, and ensures no loan disburses without human sign-off.

Built on the langgraph-clj StateGraph runtime, the same portable actor pattern powering nine other cloud-itonami verticals. Covers the core governed lifecycle from intake through underwriting approval with an immutable audit ledger, ready to fork and operate in any jurisdiction.
